## The problem

A customer walks onto your forecourt at 8pm. The showroom is shut. They're stood in
front of a car they might buy — and all they can see is a price.

They can't see the mileage, the MOT history, the forty photos, the finance options.
So they walk away. And you never even knew they were there.

## What CarTag Studio does

It prints a window label for every car, with a large QR code. The customer scans it
with their phone and lands **straight on that car's page on your website** — photos,
specification, finance, everything. Day or night, showroom open or shut.

And it handles the tedious part for you: **it watches your website, and the moment you
list a new car or change a price, the label prints itself.**

---

## Features

**Labels that print themselves**
Add a car to your website and its label is printing before you've walked to the printer.
Drop a price and a new label prints with the new figure. No typing, no copy-pasting,
no "I'll do it later".

**It reads your website for you**
Paste a car's link and it fills in the name, price, mileage and registration on its own.
It works on any dealer website — including the ones built with JavaScript, where ordinary
tools see nothing at all. It also knows the difference between the car's actual price and
the "£154 per month" finance figure sitting next to it.

**It watches your stock**
It finds your stock page by itself and checks it every few minutes. New car, it prints.
Price change, it prints. Car sold and removed from the site, it tidies itself up.
Nothing to remember, nothing to maintain.

**A price you can read across the forecourt**
The price scales itself to fill the label — large, bold and legible from the other side
of the car park. That is the number they are looking for.

**Six label designs**
Classic, Bold, Banner, Framed, Minimal and Compact. See them side by side and choose the
one that suits your brand.

**Your name on every label**
Your dealership name and tagline, your printer, your label size — 4×6, 3×2, Brother 62 mm,
or a custom size you enter yourself.

**A record of every label printed**
Searchable by registration, car name or price. Reprint any of them with one click when a
label gets tatty or peels off in the rain.

**It simply runs**
Starts with the computer and sits quietly in the tray getting on with it. You will forget
it is there — until you notice the labels are always right.

## Common questions

**Will it work with my website?**
Almost certainly. It reads the same page your customers see, so if a person can read the
price, so can CarTag Studio. It has been tested against a range of dealer platforms.

**Will it work with my printer?**
Any printer your computer can already print to — Munbyn, Zebra, Brother, DYMO, or an
ordinary office printer. The label size is up to you.

**What happens if the internet drops out?**
Printing carries on as normal. It picks your website back up when the connection returns.

**Can I still print a one-off label myself?**
Yes. Paste the car's link, check the details, print.

**Could it print a hundred labels by mistake?**
No. On the first run it takes a quiet snapshot of your existing stock and prints nothing.
After that it only prints what is genuinely new or changed, and there is a safety limit
on top of that.

**Will it slow my computer down?**
No. It sits in the tray using very little, and wakes every few minutes to check your
stock page.
