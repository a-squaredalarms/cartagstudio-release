## The problem

A customer walks onto your forecourt at 8pm. The showroom is shut. They're stood in
front of a car they might buy — and all they can see is a price.

They can't see the mileage, the MOT history, the 40 photos, the finance options.
So they walk away. And you never even knew they were there.

## What CarTag Studio does

It prints a **window label for every car**, with a big **QR code**. The customer scans
it with their phone and lands **straight on that car's page on your website** — photos,
spec, finance, the lot. Day or night, showroom open or shut.

And it does the boring part for you: **it watches your website, and the moment you list
a new car or change a price, the label prints itself.**

## ✨ Features

### 🖨️ Labels that print themselves
Add a car to your website → **its label is printing before you've walked to the printer.**
Drop a price → **a new label with the new price prints automatically.**
No typing. No copy-pasting. No "I'll do it later".

### 🔍 It reads your website for you
Paste a car's link and it fills in the name, price, mileage and registration on its own.
It works on **any dealer website** — even the ones that build their pages with JavaScript,
where ordinary tools see nothing at all. It also knows the difference between the car's
real price and the "£154 per month" finance figure sitting right next to it.

### 👀 It watches your stock
It finds your stock page by itself and checks it every few minutes.
New car? **Prints.** Price change? **Prints.** Car sold and gone from the site?
**It tidies itself up.** Nothing to remember, nothing to maintain.

### 💷 A price you can read across the forecourt
The price scales itself to fill the label — big, black and readable from the other side
of the car park. Because that's the number they're looking for.

### 🎨 Six label designs
Classic · Bold · Banner · Framed · Minimal · Compact.
See them side by side and pick the one that suits your brand.

### 🏷️ Your name on every label
Your dealership name and tagline, your printer, your label size — 4×6, 3×2,
Brother 62 mm, or a custom size you type in yourself.

### 📜 Every label you've ever printed
Searchable by registration, car name or price. Reprint any of them with one click when
a label goes tatty or peels off in the rain.

### 🌙 It just runs
Starts with the computer and sits quietly in the tray getting on with it.
You'll forget it's even there — until you notice the labels are always right.

## ❓ Common questions

**Will it work with my website?**
Almost certainly. It reads the same page your customers see — so if a person can read
the price, so can CarTag Studio. It's been tested against a range of dealer platforms.

**Will it work with my printer?**
Any printer your computer can already print to — Munbyn, Zebra, Brother, DYMO, or even
a plain office printer. The label size is up to you.

**What if the internet drops out?**
Printing carries on as normal. It picks your website back up when the connection returns.

**Can I still print a one-off label myself?**
Of course. Paste the car's link, check the details, hit print.

**Could it print a hundred labels by mistake?**
No. On day one it takes a quiet snapshot of your existing stock and prints **nothing**.
After that it only prints what is genuinely **new** or **changed** — and there's a safety
limit on top of that.

**Does it slow my computer down?**
No. It sits in the tray using next to nothing, and wakes up every few minutes to check
your stock page.
